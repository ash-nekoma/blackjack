ARCADE RULES

BLACKJACK MECHANICS
  • 1-Seat Limit: Occupy one seat per room.
  • Minimum Bet: 1,000 credits.
  • 15s Action Timer: 15 seconds to Hit/Stand/Double.
  • Payouts: Standard wins pay 1:1. Blackjack pays 3:2. Pushes refund the bet.
  • Dealer Rules: Dealer must stand on all 17s.
HIGH-LOW DICE
  • Global Betting: Everyone bets on the same two dice.
  • Under 7: Pays 1:1 if total is 2 through 6.
  • Over 7: Pays 1:1 if total is 8 through 12.
  • Exactly 7: The Jackpot. Pays 4:1 if exactly 7.
GLOBAL COIN FLIP
  • Global Betting: 15-second intervals to place bets on Heads or Tails.
  • Payouts: Winning the flip doubles your bet (Pays 1:1).
ECONOMY & SYSTEM
  • Banking Limits: Deposit: 10k-100k. Withdraw: 50k-100k.
  • Daily Rewards: Available every 24 hours.
