ARCADE RULES & MECHANICS

BLACKJACK

Beat the Dealer's hand without exceeding 21. Face cards are 10, Aces are 1 or 11.

  • Limits: Max 1 seat. Max bet 100,000 credits.
  • Timer: Exactly 15s to Hit, Stand, Double, or Split.
  • Payouts: Wins pay 1:1. Blackjack pays 3:2. Push refunds bet.
HIGH-LOW DICE

Two 6-sided dice are rolled globally. Select your bet amount and click a tile.

  • Limits: Max bet 50,000 credits per tile.
  • Under 7 / Over 7: Pays 1:1.
  • Exactly 7: Jackpot! Pays massive 4:1 odds.
8-BIT DERBY SPRINT

Bet on the winning pixel horse across a 6-lane sprint.

  • Limits: Max bet 50,000 per lane.
  • Payouts: Varies strictly by lane probability (2x up to 10x).
CLASSIC COLOR GAME

3 colored dice are rolled. Tap colors to place chips.

  • Limits: Max bet 50,000 credits per color.
  • Payouts: 1 Match = 1:1 | 2 Matches = 2:1 | 3 Matches = 3:1
PVP ARENA (WHEEL & COIN)

1v1 duels against other players for credits or items.

  • Host Powers: Host chooses Coin Flip or Custom Wheel Spin, wager amount, and formats (Best of 1, 3, or 5).
  • Custom Wheel: Host selects how many slices (2, 4, 8, 10). The wheel automatically populates with both players' names alternating!
SYSTEM RULES
  • Deposits/Withdrawals: Max limit should be 100,000 per transaction.
  • Daily Rewards: Mystery boxes respawn exactly every 24 hours.
  • Spam Protection: Arcade buttons lock for 250ms after clicking.

TERMS OF SERVICE

By requesting access to Casino Royale, you agree to the Fair Play Policy:

- Maximum of ONE active seat per room.
- Using macros or alt-accounts to farm rewards is strictly prohibited and results in an IP Ban.
- All accounts require manual Admin approval.
