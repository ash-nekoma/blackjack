ARCADE RULES & MECHANICS

BLACKJACK

Beat the Dealer's hand without exceeding 21. Face cards are 10, Aces are 1 or 11.

  • Limits: Max 1 seat. Max bet 50,000 credits (Splitting doubles this to 100k).
  • Timer: Exactly 15s to Hit, Stand, Double, or Split.
  • Payouts: Wins pay 1:1. Blackjack pays 3:2. Push refunds bet.
  • Dealer: Must draw to 16 and stand on all 17s.
HIGH-LOW DICE

Two 6-sided dice are rolled globally. Select your bet amount and click a tile.

  • Limits: Max bet 50,000 credits per tile.
  • Under 7: Pays 1:1 (Rolls 2, 3, 4, 5, 6).
  • Over 7: Pays 1:1 (Rolls 8, 9, 10, 11, 12).
  • Exactly 7: Jackpot! Pays massive 4:1 odds.
GLOBAL COIN FLIP

A single global coin is flipped. Bet on Heads or Tails before the 15s timer runs out.

  • Limits: Max bet 50,000 credits per tile.
  • Payouts: Correct guesses double your money (Pays 1:1).
CLASSIC COLOR GAME

3 colored dice are rolled. Pick your bet amount at the top, then tap colors to place chips.

  • Limits: Max bet 50,000 credits per color.
  • 1 Match: Pays 1:1
  • 2 Matches: Pays 2:1
  • 3 Matches: Pays 3:1
